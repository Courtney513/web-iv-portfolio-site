Bombas Mobile App

Interactive Design

Bombas is a online producer and distributor of athletic socks. They are dedicated to making quality socks and providing them for people in need, so they donate a pair for each pair they sell. The app is targeted towards current tech savvy customers between the ages of twenty-five to thirty. They love their Bombas socks and are looking for an easier way to purchase them.

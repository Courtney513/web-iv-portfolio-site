On Thin Ice Campaign

Social Activism

Polar Bears International is the only conservation organization who only focuses on Polar Bears. Their mission is to protect the Polar Bears and preserve the ice they live on. Polar Bears International wants to spread awareness about the dangerous conditions Polar Bears face and inspire action to aid their mission. To help spread awareness, the campaign is targeting young professionals from ages twenty-five to thirty-five. These young professionals are dedicated to helping make a positive change in the world.

Cub Grub

Identity Design

Cub Grub is an infant and toddler nutrition company that began in 2000. Their focus is providing Children with healthy food options. Their competition includes beech nut and happy family organics. Both company are in the market of healthy and natural baby food. The target audience includes younger mothers between the ages of twenty-five to thirty. These mothers are looking for affordable, natural food for their children. to appeal to the mothers the use of minimal illustrations and warm colors.
